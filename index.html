Overview

We align modern cloud architecture with measurable business outcomes. From the first migration to multi-cloud scale, we bring pragmatic guidance, secure defaults, and cost-aware patterns so you can ship faster with confidence.

Competencies: Cloud Networking, Security & Identity, Migrations, Cloud Cost Management (FinOps), Application & Web Hosting, Content Delivery, and Big Data Pipelines.

Platforms: Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP).

Services

Cloud Strategy & Migrations

Assessments, roadmaps, and landing zones. Lift-and-shift, re-platforming, and re-architecture. Hybrid and multi-cloud connectivity with VPN, Direct Connect, and ExpressRoute.

Networking, Security & Identity

  • Zero-trust patterns, network segmentation, VPC/VNet design
  • IAM design & SSO, secrets & key management, governance guardrails
  • WAF, threat detection, posture management, policy-as-code

Cost Optimization (FinOps)

  • Rightsizing & scheduling, Savings Plans/Reservations strategy
  • Usage analytics & tagging hygiene, budget guardrails & alerts
  • Showback/chargeback, unit economics, KPI dashboards

App & Web Hosting + Content Delivery

  • Containers & serverless, CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure-as-code
  • Global CDN, caching strategy, performance tuning
  • Reliability: SLOs, autoscaling, health checks, graceful deploys

Data Pipelines & Analytics

  • Data lakes, ETL/ELT, streaming pipelines, event-driven architectures
  • Warehouse modeling, BI enablement, data governance
  • Observability: lineage, quality checks, cost & performance KPIs

Need something not listed? We tailor engagements from a focused 1–2 week assessment to ongoing hands-on partnership.
